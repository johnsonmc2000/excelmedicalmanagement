Why practices work with EMM

About Excel Medical Management

Excel Medical Management (EMM) is a medical billing and revenue cycle management company built to help healthcare practices get paid accurately and on time. EMM manages the day-to-day billing workload — claims, follow-up, denials, and payment posting — so practices can spend less time on administrative burden and more time on patient care.

EMM's team brings hands-on experience across commercial billing and workers' compensation billing, giving practices a partner who understands both standard payer requirements and the added complexity of occupational injury claims.

What sets EMM apart

  • Dedicated follow-up on aging claims and denials
  • Workers' comp billing expertise, including DaisyBill
  • Transparent reporting on revenue performance
  • Responsive, practice-first communication
